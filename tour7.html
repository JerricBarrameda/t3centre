Cross over to Guimaras for island views, lighthouses, mango farms, and cultural stops before heading to Bacolod for ancestral homes, plazas, and the famous Ruins. Capped with leisure time at Campuestohan Highland Resort, this trip offers the perfect mix of history, culture, and relaxation.
