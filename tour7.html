The B-I-G Tour Packages (Bacolod - Iloilo - Guimaras)

Experience the B-I-G Tour Package (Bacolod, Iloilo, Guimaras) — a 5-day journey through the cultural and natural gems of Western Visayas. Explore heritage landmarks, ancestral houses, stunning resorts, and island escapes while savoring local favorites like Chicken Inasal and La Paz Batchoy. With guided tours, smooth transfers, and well-planned itineraries, this package offers the perfect mix of history, adventure, and relaxation.

2025 5D4N BACOLOD ILOILO GUIMARAS TOUR PACKAGE

This 5 Day and 4 Nights B-I-G Tour Package (Bacolod, Iloilo, Guimaras) takes you on a cultural and scenic adventure across three iconic destinations in Western Visayas. Explore ancestral houses, heritage churches, mountain resorts, island escapes, and savor local delicacies like Chicken Inasal and La Paz Batchoy. With guided tours, seamless transfers, and curated experiences, this trip lets you enjoy history, nature, and flavors all in one journey.

Tour Highlights

The good life personified in the heritage houses of Negros would often take centerstage when it comes to Talisay and Silay Tours, however, we shall take culture and heritage farther with hands on activity in Casa Gamboa or inside the Silay City Art Space and Showroom (Old Silay Puericulture Center). Hand painting of Calatrava bags or single wire arts or stone art painting in the afternoon and interaction with local artists.

9:00 Start of the tour
