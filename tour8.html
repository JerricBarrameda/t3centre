Half Day Tour Packages

Make the most of your time with our half-day tours in Iloilo and Bacolod—perfect for travelers who want to see the highlights without spending the whole day. Join our Iloilo half-day tours and explore historic Spanish-era churches, stroll along the Iloilo Esplanade, and taste the iconic La Paz Batchoy. Over in Bacolod, our half-day trips take you to The Ruins, let you savor the city’s famous chicken inasal, and immerse you in its rich culture and artistry. Whether you’re here for a quick getaway or stopping over on a cruise, our Iloilo and Bacolod tours are designed to give you the best experiences in just a few hours. ✨ Don’t miss out—book your half-day adventure today and create unforgettable memories!
