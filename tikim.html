Tikim & Timpla: A Bacolod Food & Crafts Crawl

A perfectly seasoned day of tasting, making, and discovering the soulful heart of Negros.

Tour Highlights

You can’t leave Bacolod without tasting its famous flavors. Tikim means “to taste” and Timpla is “to mix or craft,” making this perfect for foodies and creative souls. Enjoy cansi soup, napoleones, ensaimadas, and participate in hands-on workshops.

Itinerary:

9:00 Pick up Hotel

9:30 Running tour of Bacolod City, tasting piaya

10:30 Visit NVC Showroom with local art and upcycled creations

11:00 Taste Cansi, a local beef bone broth

1:00 Napoleones & Ensaymada tasting

2:30 Explore Manokan Country for local chicken delicacies

4:00 Handmade Gallery workshop

5:30 Slow Food degustation at Lanai by Fresh Start

What to wear: Comfortable clothes
