About Us

T3 Trips Tickets & Tours Centre is a Department of Tourism (DOT) accredited travel agency that operates tours in and around Bacolod, Iloilo & Guimaras. We offer packages for incentive & educational tours, field trips, LGU programs, and transportation rentals. It is our business where you sleep, eat, learn and have fun. Feel free to explore our site or contact us at t3centre@yahoo.com.
